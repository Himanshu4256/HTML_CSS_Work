Why do we celebrate Engineer's Day?

Father of Engneers is M.Visvesvaraya Different parts of the country celebrate Engineer's Day to appreciate the contribution of engineers. India observes Engineer's Day on September 15 as a mark of remembrance and tribute to Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, who is considered as one of the greatest engineers in the country. Engineer's Day is observed in several countries on various dates of the year. As of 20 November 2019, the World Federation of Engineering Organizations (WFEO) has designated March 4th as 'World Engineering Day for a Sustainable World'
